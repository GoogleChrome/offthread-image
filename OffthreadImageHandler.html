Class: OffthreadImageHandler

OffthreadImageHandler

The coordinator for all OffthreadImage instances. Spins up the worker and has it enqueue image loads by URL. When an ImageBitmap is returned from the worker it notifies all OffthreadImages. It is also responsible for setting the status of all OffthreadImages, i.e. loaded, decoded, etc.
